Emergency Contacts

Quick access to essential local and national helplines for urgent support.

Emergency Services Call 112 or your local emergency number immediately.
Medical Helpline Contact your nearest hospital or ambulance service for urgent care.
Women and Child Support Reach out to local women’s welfare offices or child protection services.
Community Shelter Visit the nearest local shelter or community center for short-term assistance.

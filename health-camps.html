Health Camp Information

Learn about free health checkups, vaccination drives, and wellness events in your neighborhood.

Services Often Available

  • Blood pressure and sugar testing
  • Vaccination drives
  • Eye and dental screening
  • Nutrition and wellness counseling

What to Bring

  • Identity proof
  • Medical history if available
  • Any prescriptions
  • Comfortable clothing
